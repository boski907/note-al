Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Notematica stores account data, notes, flashcards, and billing references required to provide the service.

We use third-party providers for functionality:

- Supabase for authentication and database storage.

- OpenAI for AI note, flashcard, test-prep, and transcription features.

- Stripe for subscription billing and customer portal.

- Google AdSense for advertising on free plans.

We collect product analytics events (for example: save note, generate flashcards, start checkout) to improve reliability and UX.

Users on active ad-free subscriptions should not see ads. Free users may see ads.

For requests regarding your data, contact support at your published support email.
